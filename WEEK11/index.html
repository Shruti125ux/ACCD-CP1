Fall Ecosystem

An interactive fall ecosystem simulation featuring a tree that drops leaves with realistic physics, adjustable wind speed, and seasonal transformations between fall and summer.

Inspiration

Looking outside my window daily and seeing the fall colors and trees and the changing seasons and falling of leaves.

Design Process

Initial Explorations

Moving around and exploring the background color and representation of ground and tree with rectangle and circle.

Adding Details

Detailing of the circle for it to look more like a tree. Added more circles and changed their colors to resemble a fall tree.

Adding Features

Added the buttons, its colors, a slider, leaves falling, change of season. Added Class Leaf for the leaves animation.

The image shows that the season can be changed from Fall to Summer.
